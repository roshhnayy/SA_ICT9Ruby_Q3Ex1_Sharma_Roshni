Aurora

Aurora, also referred to as Sleeping Beauty, is a well-known fairy tale that has been passed down through various cultural traditions. The story centers around a beautiful princess named Aurora who is cursed by an evil fairy to fall into a deep sleep after pricking her finger on a spindle, and can only be awakened by true love's kiss.

Marie Aristocrat

Marie is a character in "The Aristocats," a Disney movie about cats. She's a sweet white kitten who, along with her brothers, goes on an adventure to find her way back home after facing some challenges.

Cinderella

Cinderella is a classic fairy tale character who faces challenges from her stepfamily but finds hope through her fairy godmother. With her help, she attends a royal ball, where she meets the prince and falls in love, ultimately transforming her life for the better.

Silvermist

Silvermist is a water fairy in the Tinker Bell movies known for her gentle nature and ability to manipulate water. She's caring and supportive, always ready to help her friends.
